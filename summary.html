14  Summary

This book gives general guidelines and tips to manage industry projects in courses. You will receive specific details in project briefs that will vary across clients. Therefore, read the project brief carefully to ensure you complete all the parts that may not be part of this book.

You should always contact your course instructor when in doubt. When you send an email please follow the following rules.

  • Mention course and project group in the subject line (e.g., Marketing Analytics: Group-3)
  • In the body of the email, ask questions in points not in paragraphs. Limit your points to five.
  • Include all team membersâ€™ email ids in the CC field.

Here is an example of an email.

Hi Ashish,

I have following doubts regarding the project report.

- How many pages are required?
- Do you expect us to include an appendix in the report?

Cheers,
Group-6

I prefer to address your questions point by point so that there is much clarity. For example, my response to your above email will likely be something like this:

Hello Group-6,
Please see my replies in-lined. 
If you have any further questions, feel free to contact me. 
Regards,
Ashish 

> Hi Ashish,

> I have following doubts regarding the project report.
>
> - How many pages are required?

A typical report that looks professional is between 15-20 pages. 
If you exceed 20 pages, you will not be penalzied. 

> - Do you expect us to include an appendix in the report?

No. However, if you have additional information that you like to include 
in the report without breaking the flow, please add them in appendix.

> Cheers,
> Group-6
